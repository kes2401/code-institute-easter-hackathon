About Us

At Eggstatic Events we host fun, educational and exciting Easter events for the whole family.

Based on MacDonalds Farm it is the perfect Easter venue for all the family.

Join us over the Easter holidays at the farm for our five-day Easter extravaganza
Events take place every day from 8:30am to 5:30pm with activities for the children of all ages.
Leading up to the Big Easter Egg Hunt on the last day.

Keeping children entertained over the Easter period is the greatest challenge faced by parents. The chocolate, the sugar highs, the tantrums, the stress…
While the children are running off their excess energy in a safe, educational environment, you can relax and enjoy lunch in our restaurant where we serve only locally sourced organic food.

MacDonalds farm is a working farm enabling children to experience real farm life and up close with all the animals.
All our fun, friendly and enthusiastic staff are qualified and registered childminders.
So you know that your children will be safe and having fun.

Come join us at Eggstatic Events where we have everything to ensure you will have an egg-cellent Easter Holiday.
